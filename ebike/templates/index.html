How To get started?

  1. Login your account.
  2. Locate many available vehicle nearby using the map function in the website.
  3. Top-up into your account.
  4. Press the "start" button to finish your journey.
  5. Press the "End" button to terminate your ride and report the status of the vehicle.

In the UK, you must be at least 18 years old and hold a provisional or full driving licence to ride our scooters.

Ride With Care

  • Be sure to know local rules of the road before using a scooter.
  • Stay off pavements.
  • Riding on the bike lanes as possible, or close to the side curb.
  • Be careful of intersections.
  • Ride with two hands and leave your headphones on until you reach your destination.
  • If you want to use your phone to navigate, put it on a stand.

Parking with care

  • Park correctly to help keep the pavement accessible for all.
  • Place the scooter on the pavement, close to a wall or in a designated parking area.
  • Use the kickstand to ensure the scooter and bike are upright.
  • Ensure that you do not block any access ramps, bus stops, pedestrian crossings or entrances, or obstruct public areas in any other way.
  • Always observe local parking rules
